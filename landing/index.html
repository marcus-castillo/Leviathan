Leviathan

Judicial opinion analysis suite — bias metrics, dataset builder, citation graph
Note — interpretation guardrail. Leviathan reports statistical patterns in text, outcomes, and citations only. It does not measure judicial intent, ideology, or bias, and cannot establish causation. Every figure ships with confidence, sample-size, and limitations caveats. Treat all results as hypotheses for qualified human review — never as conclusions about any person.
